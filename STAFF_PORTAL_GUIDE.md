# 📄 Chocoberry Staff Guide: How to Upload Invoices

**GOAL**: No more WhatsApp photos. Every invoice must be uploaded through the portal immediately after the delivery arrives.

---

### 📲 Step 1: Access the Portal
1. Join the **Shop WiFi**.
2. Open your phone camera and scan the QR code (if provided) OR type this link into your phone browser:
   **`http://<YOUR_IP_HERE>:5050`**

### 📸 Step 2: Take the Photo
1. Tap the **"Camera"** icon on the screen.
2. Photograph the invoice page that shows the **Total Amount**.
3. Make sure the **Supplier Name**, **Date**, and **Total** are clear.

### 🤖 Step 3: AI Check
* The AI will try to read the price and supplier name for you.
* Check the fields on the screen! If they are wrong, fix them manually.
* Enter your name so we know who accepted the delivery.

### ✅ Step 4: Submit
* Tap **"Submit Invoice"**. 
* Once you see the green checkmark, you are done. Put the paper invoice in the physical folder as usual.

---

### 💡 Tips for Success
* **One Delivery = One Upload**. Do not wait until the end of the shift.
* **Keep it Flat**. Take the photo from directly above so the AI can read it.
* **Bad Lighting?** Use your flash if the shop is dim.
